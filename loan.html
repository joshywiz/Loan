Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our loans

To qualify for a LoanEase personal loan, you must: be at least 18 years old, have a valid government-issued ID, provide proof of income (minimum $2,000/month), have an active bank account, and have a credit score of at least 600. Business loans have additional requirements including time in business and annual revenue.

Our online application takes about 5-10 minutes to complete. After submission, most applicants receive a decision within 24 hours. Once approved, funds are typically deposited into your bank account within 1-2 business days.

We offer personal loans from $5,000 up to $100,000. The exact amount you qualify for depends on factors like your credit score, income, debt-to-income ratio, and loan purpose. Business loans may be available for higher amounts with additional documentation.

No, there are no prepayment penalties with LoanEase. You can pay off your loan early at any time without additional fees, which can save you money on interest.

We offer several convenient payment options: automatic bank transfers (ACH), online payments through your LoanEase account, checks by mail, or in-person payments at our partner locations. You can also set up autopay to ensure you never miss a payment.
